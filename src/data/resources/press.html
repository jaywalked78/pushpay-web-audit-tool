Industry Commentary

For churches that also want to develop their engagement with their congregation, Pushpay offers both donation development and delivering tools as well as community management and engagement software.

Users love Pushpay because of its mobile-first approach, which is perfect for the modern giver.

In addition to the features you’d expect in a mobile giving app, Pushpay’s website also touts a management tool: their ‘Donor Development’ feature. PushPay does strong work in connecting people’s stories with the nuts-and-bolts of giving and mobile security.

I’ve been watching the Pushpay journey from the beginning. In fact, back in the day, no one knew what digital giving was. Pushpay was the first. Since then, there have been many companies to enter the market. But Pushpay has always been the trailblazer. So great to see that there’s still innovation coming from your team.
